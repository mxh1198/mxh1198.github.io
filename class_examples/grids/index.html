Street food and Restaurants

Tokyo is very proud of its famous street food. All over Tokyo, you will find little street venders selling all kinds of traditional Japanese food. Traditional japanese street food include dishes such as okonomiyaki which are like savory pancakes, takoyaki, yakisoba, and many more dishes. The top 3 places in Tokyo to buy the best streetfood include Asakusa (which is a historic area of Tokyo's shitamach which served as the city's most popular entertainment disctrict during the Edo period), Sunamachi Ginza Shopping Streer (this is a 670 meter long shopping district that is full of different types of street food), and finally Ameyoko Market (a 164,227 square-foot open air market)

Sites and Culture

There are many famous sites and landmarks in Tokyo, both traditional Japanese, and modern day attractions. The Senso-ji is an ancient budhist temple located in Asakusa, Tokyo. The famous Tokyo Tower is the second tallest structure in Japan. It is an Eiffel tower inspired tower. Harajuku is a famous district in Tokyo and is known worldwide as a center of Japanese youth culture and Fashion.

Visit Tokyo

The Aman Hotel

The Aman hotel located in the Otemachi Tower, is one of Tokyo's top hotels. The Aman hotel has won many Luxury Hotel Awards. Occupying the top six floors of the Otemachi Tower, it reflects the city's cosmopolitan personality and Japanese soul. It holds spectacular views of the Imperial Gardens, Tokyo Skytree and Mount Fuji.
