How to make a berry smoothie

Utensils Ingredients Instructions

Utensils needed:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1 banana
  • TSP honey
  • TSP lemon juice
  • TBSP peanut butter
  • strawberries (for topping)
  • granola (for topping)
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. cut up banana into small pieces
  2. put all ingredients in blender (except toppings)
  3. blend until the smoothie is the consistency you like
  4. pour smoothie into a bowl (or a glass)
  5. cut up strawberries into slices
  6. top smoothie with strawberries and granola

VOILA! The perfect berry smoothie!
