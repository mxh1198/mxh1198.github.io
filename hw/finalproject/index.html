HEALTHY RECIPE:

Chia seed pudding is the perfect healthy and easy breakfast, with a number of health benefits. Chia seeds are high in protein and are rich in fibre.If you're plant-based, chia seeds contain healthy omega-3 and are a great source of iron, which you may be missing if you're not eating meat.

CHIA SEED PUDDING:

What you need:

  • 2 TBSP chia seeds
  • Half a cup dairy/non dairy milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Berries
  • Kiwi fruit

Directions:

  • Mix chia seeds and milk thoroughly in a jar
  • Let sit for 10 minutes, then mix again
  • Cover and refrigarate overnight
  • Once its ready to eat, prepare berries and kiwi for topping and add to the pudding

WORKOUTS:

These two workouts are designed to tone and sculpt the abs, legs, and arms. These workouts will make you feel stronger and healthier and will also give you more muscle defintion if done consistently. Push yourself as much as you are comfortable with, and take breaks if needed!

AB WORKOUT:

  1. Crunch 30 reps x 2
  2. Legs at 90 crunch 30 reps x 2
  3. Half Pike ups 20 reps x 2
  4. Side crunch righ 30 reps x 2
  5. Side crunch left 30 reps x 2
  6. Heel taps 40 reps x 2
  7. Plank hip dips 20 reps x 2
  8. Plank 30 seconds x 2
  9. Side plank right 30 seconds x 2
  10. Side plank hip dips right 20 reps x 2
  11. Side plank left 30 seconds x 2
  12. Side plank hip dips left 20 reps x 2
  13. Tuck overs 15 reps x 2
  14. Pike overs 15 reps x 2
  15. Dish hold 30 seconds x 2

LEG + ARM WORKOUT:

  1. Squats x10
  2. tricep dips x10
  3. Lunges x10 each side
  4. Walk out with shoulder tap x10
  5. Bulgarian squat x10 each side
  6. Push up x10
  7. Squat jump x10
  8. Bicep curl x5 each side
    9. Repeat circuit 3 times
