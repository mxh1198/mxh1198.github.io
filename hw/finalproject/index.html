CHIA SEED PUDDING:

What you need:

  • 2 TBSP chia seeds
  • Half a cup dairy/non dairy milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Berries
  • Kiwi fruit

Directions:

  • Mix chia seeds and milk thoroughly in a jar
  • Let sit for 10 minutes, then mix again
  • Cover and refrigarate overnight
  • Once its ready to eat, prepare berries and kiwi for topping and add to the pudding
