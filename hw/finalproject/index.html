This page will give you all the ingredients and directions needed to make the best berry smoothie!

Healthy recipe:

  • Blender
  • Knife
  • Spoon
  • Chopping board

Ab workout:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1 TBSP yogurt
  • 1 TSP honey
  • I TBSP peanut butter
  • Granola (for topping)

Leg workout:

  1. Cut up banana into small pieces (half of the banana will go in the smoothie, half is for the topping)
  2. Place all ingredients in blender, except the granola and the other half of the banana.
  3. Blend until the smoothie is your desired consistency
  4. pour smoothie into a bowl or glass
  5. Top smoothie with half the sliced banana and granola, or any other toppings you prefer
  6. Enjoy!
